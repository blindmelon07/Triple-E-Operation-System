Charge Transaction

User Guide  ·  POS Module

Overview

A Charge transaction is a credit sale where the customer takes the goods now and pays at a later date based on an agreed payment term. No cash is collected at the time of the transaction.

Important: Charge transactions are recorded in the system but will not appear in any daily, register, or period report until the customer's payment has been collected and the record is updated to Paid.

How to Add a Charge Transaction

1
Add Items to the Cart

Select the products the customer is taking. Adjust quantities as needed. Inventory will be deducted immediately when the sale is confirmed.

2
Select the Customer

Charge transactions must always be linked to a named customer. Use the customer selector at the top of the cart panel. If the customer does not exist yet, click + Add Customer to create one on the spot.

3
Click "Charge / Pay"

Click the Charge / Pay button at the bottom of the cart to open the payment modal.

4
Select "Charge" as the Payment Method

In the Payment Method dropdown, choose Charge. The Payment Terms selector will appear automatically below the dropdown.

5
Select the Payment Term

Choose the number of days the customer has to settle the balance. The due date is calculated and displayed automatically.

Term Due Date Typical Use
5 DaysToday + 5 daysShort-term / regular customers
10 DaysToday + 10 daysShort-term credit
15 DaysToday + 15 daysSemi-monthly terms

60 DaysToday + 60 daysExtended credit accounts
6
Confirm the Transaction

Click Confirm Payment. The sale is saved and inventory is reduced. The transaction is tagged as Unpaid with ₱0.00 amount paid.

What Happens After Confirming

  • The sale is saved with payment_status = Unpaid and amount_paid = ₱0.00.
  • The due date is set based on today's date plus the selected term.
  • Inventory is deducted immediately.
  • The transaction does not count toward register session totals (no cash changed hands).
  • The transaction will not appear in any report (Daily, Register Closure, or Period Report) until it is marked as Paid.
  • The sale is visible in the Admin Panel → Sales with a filter by payment status.

How to Collect Payment & Close the Charge

Once the customer settles their balance, update the transaction in the admin panel:

1
Go to Admin Panel → Sales

Navigate to the Sales section in the admin panel.

2
Find the Charge Transaction

Filter by Payment Method: Charge or Payment Status: Unpaid to locate the record.

3
Update the Record

Open the sale and set:

  • Payment Status → Paid
  • Amount Paid → the amount collected
  • Paid Date → the date payment was received
4
Save

Once saved, the transaction will automatically be included in all reports going forward.

After marking as Paid: The transaction will appear in the Daily Transaction Report, Register Closure Report, and Period Report under the session it was originally recorded in.

Key Rules & Reminders

  • Always assign a customer. Charge sales must be tied to a named account for proper accounts receivable tracking.
  • Reports exclude unpaid charges. Charge transactions only appear in reports once marked as Paid.
  • Inventory is deducted at sale time. Regardless of payment status, stock is reduced immediately when the charge is confirmed.
  • No cash is added to the register. Charge sales do not count toward the session's cash totals or transaction count.
  • Monitor overdue accounts regularly via Admin Panel → Sales, filtering by Unpaid status and checking due dates.
Reminder: Do not use Charge for walk-in customers without a proper account. Always verify the customer's credit standing before approving a charge transaction.
